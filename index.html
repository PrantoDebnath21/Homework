No in-person class at JU from Jan 9

The Daily Star

Jahangirnagar University (JU) authorities have decided to suspend in-person classes from Sunday (January 9) due to the present Covid-19 situation.

JU's acting registrar Rahima Kaneez confirmed to The Daily Star today.

The decision will remain effective till further notice but the offices will remain open and administrative activities will continue
